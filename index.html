THE ADVENTURE IS IN THREE ACTS

ACT1: PRE-VISIT

The adventure begins in the classroom with a standards-rich, digitally driven roleplay experience. An anomaly has been detected and its up to the Avengers recruits to solve the mystery. Choose their pathway: life science, physical science, or earth science. In the life science pathway, you and your team of scientist recruits must investigate and classify any potential life forms and determine the level of threat they pose. By successfully achieving all of the learning outcomes you and your students qualify for official training at the S.T.A.T.I.O.N. facility.
